Code Monkey Projectiles

A blog by Timothy Klenke

I've been blogging since 2013. It started as a New Year's resolution to blog twice a month for 2014. I made it to September, which is better than most of my other New Year's resolutions.

Now I just blog when I get the urge. Usually a couple times a year. The tag line for the blog is "Code Monkey Projectiles". Please browse the archive.
